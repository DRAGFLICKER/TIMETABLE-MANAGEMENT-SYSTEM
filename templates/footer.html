Voice Field

Voice authentication uses voice biometrics to identify individuals by their unique speech characteristics, called voiceprints. However, it's vulnerable to spoofing attacks, where attackers trick the system with pre-recorded or synthetic voice samples. Researchers are improving security by analyzing speech details and adding factors like behavioral patterns to tell apart real users from imposters. .
